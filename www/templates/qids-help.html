What do symptom scores mean?

A score will be calculated if you answer all questions for a symtom record.

A higher score indicates that you are experiencing more symptoms of depression.

The maximum score is 27 and the minimum score is 0. If you wish to share your responses with family, friends or health professionals you can choose to do so yourself.

If you think your score is high or you are worried about your symptoms worsening, contact your GP or other contacts listed in the help and support section.
